UUCSE Official Team 2022

According to the previous announcement published on the formation of UUCSE Official Programming team, the first individual programming contest was held on August 8, 2022. The second contest was scheduled on August 15, 2022 but as the day was our National Mourning day and hence the department observed it with showing full respect. As we have not found any alternative day to organize the contest, the UUCSE Official Programming team is formed according to the leader board of the first individual contest with close mentor's observation.

So, congratulation guys. The first three individual will form the team. The name of the team will be UU_Flag when it represents the department. The department will officially patronize the team. But the department encourages other teams to participate in contests. The training sessions are for all participants.

The team members of UU_Flag is NOT fixed. Very soon we will publish a pool of individual contestants. We will update the list of team members from the pool. The pool itself will be updated with the progress of the individual contestants. Thank you.
