Individual Evaluation Contests for ICPC Preliminary 2022

Dear students,
The Department of CSE, UU is going to organize two successive programming contest
with a vision to harness a competitive programming culture among the students. To create
a competitive environment with a goal to improve in the contest ranking the department
has set the following initiatives:

  • The department will form its official proramming team/s according to individual performance.
  • Only the official team/s will be financially patronized by the department.
  • The department encourages each and every UU CSE student to participate in the training session.
  • The department has a vision to create a strong girl's programming environment with a focus to have a better ranking in girls programiming contests.


    • Along with these long term goals, the UUCSE is looking for a good rank in upcomming ICPC regionals.
    With that vision it is announcing the following schedules:
  • The first individual contest will be held at Monday, 8 August, 2022.
  • The second individual contest will be held at Monday, 15 August, 2022.
  • The team will be formed according to individual performance.
    • \
  • Both first and second contest will take place in LAB 10 and LAB 9 if needed. Time duration is 3 hours.
  • The contests will be started at 10 AM and the selected platform is Hackerrank.
